Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is your return and exchange policy?

A: We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase for items that are unused and in their original packaging. Please contact our customer service team to initiate the return or exchange process.

Q: What should I do if my order arrived damaged or defective?

A: We apologize for any inconvenience. Please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your order and provide photos of the damage or defect. We will work with you to provide a solution, such as a replacement or refund.

Q: How long does it take to process a return or exchange?

A: Once we receive the returned item and verify its condition, we will process the refund or exchange within 3-5 business days.
