Operation Manual

Items in Package: Main product, joystick, microphone, two type-C wires.

1. Open the package and check if there is any broken.

2. connect the joystick to the five female-DuPont wires and connect the microphone to the Voice Recognition board.

3. Check the connection of all DuPont wires, be careful about the VCC and gound and connect the two type-C interface to the power.

4. The green light indicates the device works correctly, while the blue light indicates the connection between the Voice Recognition V3 Board and the breadboard is lost and need to reconnect.

5. You should now see the temperature, humidity, the lightness of the night light and the state of the Hutao Shake Toy.

6. To use the joystick, up and down are used to switch the menu and right and left are used to change the lightness of the light and drive the HTY work or stop.

7. You can use the following voice command to control the device:

"Hutao", "Shake": drive the HTY work

"quit": drive the HTY stop

"brighten": make the night light more bright

"dark": make the night light more dark
